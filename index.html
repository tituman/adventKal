Welcome to daniel's advent calendar.

This is a random advent calendar website, that works a little differently. you can try every hour to click on the button and hope that the randomly generated number is the one corresponding to today's date. In that case, you can find out what your Christmas gift will be. Otherwise, you have to keep trying.

If you find a pass-code you can copy and paste it in the following box for decryption.

As an example, "Daniel" encodes to "RGFuaWVs".
